Account Deletion Request – Meowly

If you would like to delete your Meowly account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to our support team at kazarovnikita1@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request – Meowly".
  3. In the body of the email, please include:
    • Your registered email address
    • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account

What Happens Next

  • We will verify your request within 7 business days.
  • All your account data, including photos, profile details, and stored content, will be permanently deleted.
  • Some anonymous logs may be retained for up to 30 days for security and analytics purposes.
